Payment Information

FEMTEC is a not-for-profit conference. Registration fees were calculated to cover basic logistics, preparation and printing of conference materials, conference facilities, audio-visual services, and expenses of invited keynote speakers.

Registration fee By March 1, 2011 After March 1, 2011
Regular participants 350 USD 550 USD
Student participants 250 USD 450 USD

Registration fee is to be paid to University of Nevada, Reno. A separate fee to cover conference breakfasts, lunches and coffee breaks, as well as a Welcome Reception and Farewell Party, is paid to the Harvey's Casino and Resort together with accommodation. See the Hotel Information section for more details.

Payment Instructions

  • Payments of conference fees are routed through a central account of Bank of America to a central UNR account where they need to be identified among hundreds of other payments. Please make sure to include the name "FEMTEC 2011" in the wire details.
  • Scanned copy of your payment receipt must be sent via e-mail to femtec2011@unr.edu.
  • All bank fees have to be paid by the sender. This is particularly important when sending payment from an overseas bank.
