Payment Information

Registration fees for FEMTEC 2011 conference are as follows:

Registration fee By March 15, 2010 After March 15, 2010
Regular participants 350 EUR 500 EUR
Student participants 250 EUR 400 EUR
Accompanying persons 200 EUR 250 EUR

The regular and student fees cover all conference materials, book of abstracts, conference lunches, coffee breaks, welcome reception, farewell party, and excursion. The accompanying person fee covers conference lunches, welcome reception, farewell party, and excursion.

Payment Instructions

  • All payments are routed through a central account of Bank of America to a central UNR account where they need to be identified among thousands of other payments. Please make sure to include the name "FEMTEC" in the wire details.
  • After the payment, please scan the copy of your payment confirmation and send via e-mail to femtec2011@unr.edu. This serves as a proof of payment and helps track lost payments.
  • All bank fees have to be paid by the sender.
