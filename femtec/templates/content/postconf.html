Post Conference Program

For participants wishing to spend the weekend after the conference at Lake Tahoe, we will organize an optional hike into the heart of the Desolation Wilderness on Saturday, May 14. The Desolation Wilderness is one of the most beautiful wilderness areas in the United States.


There are many possible destinations including Echo Lakes, Eagle Lake, Lake Aloha (shown above) and others, and a final selection will be made during the conference. Note that virtually all trails in the Desolation Wilderness are covered with sand and/or loose granite, so solid hiking shoes are highly recommended. {% endblock %}
