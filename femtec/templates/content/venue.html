Conference Venue

The conference will take place at the Harvey's Casino and Resort in South Lake Tahoe, CA.


Reserving Accommodation

All FEMTEC participants need to book their accommodation with the Harvey's Casino and Resort, using a toll-free number 1-800-455-4770. There are two room options: Mountain Tower Room at $79 + tax and Lake Tower Room at $99 + tax. A $20 surcharge per guest, per room, per night will apply for each additional person over 2 people regardless of age. In order to receive these discounted prices, mention that you attend the FEMTEC 2011 conference. You will be asked to provide a group code which is S05FEMT-SN. At the time of reservation, each participant will also pay a $320 fee to cover conference breakfasts, lunches, coffee breaks, a Welcome Reception and a Farewell Party.

According to the hotel's policy, any individual cancellation within 72 hours from the date of arrival or "no shows" will be charged first night room and tax to the credit card on file. Check in time is 4:00 PM and checkout time is 12:00 noon.

The above prices are valid through March 11, 2011. After that, the room block will be cancelled and regular hotel pricing will apply. Even after March 11, all FEMTEC participants still need to book their accommodation with the Harvey's Casino and Resort.
