Conference Venue

The conference will take place in the historical conference center Secese of the Pilsner Urquell Brewery, and catering will be provided by the famous brewery restaurant Na Spilce. Pilsen is a beautiful historical city whose origins date back to the 13th century. An overview of the rich Pilsen's history can be found on its Wikipedia page. The city features many sightseeing attractions.

Part of the conference program will be optional tours of the Pilsner Urquell brewery museum, historical undergrounds of Pilsen, and St. Bartholomeus Cathedral.
