Conference Venue

The conference will take place at the Harvey's Casino and Resort in South Lake Tahoe, CA.


Hotel Information

All FEMTEC participants need to book their accommodation with the Harvey's Casino and Resort. An overview of prices is given in the following table:
Lodging and Food Cost
Mountain Tower rooms $79 + tax (per night)
Lake Tower rooms $99 + tax (per night)
Meals $320 (one time)

The food charge covers meals for the entire duration of the conference and it is to be paid to the hotel along with lodging. The meals include a breakfast buffet, lunch and coffee breaks each day, as well as a Welcome Reception on Monday and a Farewell Party on Thursday. Participants will special dietary needs can indicate this in the online registration form.

Reservations can be done using a toll-free number 1-800-455-4770. A $20 surcharge per guest, per room, per night will apply for each additional person over 2 people regardless of age. In order to receive these discounted prices, mention that you attend the FEMTEC 2011 conference. You will be asked to provide a group code which is S05FEMT-SN.

There will be free high-speed wireless Internet in the lecture rooms, and free parking in the hotel's garage.

According to the hotel's policy, any individual cancellation within 72 hours from the date of arrival or "no shows" will be charged first night room and tax to the credit card on file. Check in time is 4:00 PM and checkout time is 12:00 noon.

The above prices are valid through March 11, 2011. After that, the room block will be cancelled and regular hotel pricing will apply. Even after March 11, all FEMTEC participants still need to book their accommodation with the Harvey's Casino and Resort.
